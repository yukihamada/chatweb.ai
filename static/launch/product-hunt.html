Hey Product Hunt! I'm Yuki, founder of chatweb.ai.

Quick background: I was CPO/Director at Mercari (Japan's largest marketplace, IPO'd at $7B) and co-founded NOT A HOTEL. After years of building products that empower individuals, I noticed a fundamental gap in today's AI:

LLMs are incredibly smart, but they can't *do* anything.

Ask ChatGPT to send an email — it'll write a perfect draft. But YOU still need to open Gmail, paste it, and hit send.

chatweb.ai closes this gap with 39 specialized agents that actually execute tasks:

- **GmailAgent**: Sends, replies, organizes emails
- **WebPublisher**: Generates and publishes websites
- **CodeDeployer**: Builds and deploys your code
- **BrowserAgent**: Browses the web and extracts info
- ...and 35 more specialized agents

**What makes us different:**

1. **A2A v1.0 Protocol** — We're built on Google's Agent-to-Agent standard (JSON-RPC 2.0 + SSE streaming), so our agents truly collaborate.

2. **12 AI Models** — Not locked to one LLM. We orchestrate Llama 4 Scout, Claude Opus, GPT-4o, Gemini Pro, and more based on task type.

3. **Self-Improving Loop** — Every 6 hours, agent quality is evaluated and prompts are auto-optimized. Pro plan targets 9/10 quality.

4. **Going Open Source** — We plan to open-source the core protocol.

Free tier includes $2/month in credits. Would love your feedback!

https://chatweb.ai
